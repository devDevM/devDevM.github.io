About Deven

Deven Morone is an engineer with a passion for music, audio signal processing, AI, machine learning, electronics, and video games. With a strong background in electrical and computer engineering, Deven loves to combine his interests to create innovative projects and solutions. When not working on his latest project, you can find Deven jamming on his guitar or immersing himself in the world of video games.
